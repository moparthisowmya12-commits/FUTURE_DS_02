# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard (Task 2)

## 📊 Project Title
**Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard**

---

## 📌 Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze customer data to identify churn patterns, retention trends, and the key factors influencing customer loss. The dashboard provides actionable insights that help businesses improve customer retention and reduce churn.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Tableau
- Microsoft Excel (Telco Customer Churn Dataset)

---

## 📂 Dataset
**Dataset Name:** Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset includes information such as:
- Customer ID
- Gender
- Senior Citizen
- Partner
- Dependents
- Tenure
- Contract Type
- Payment Method
- Monthly Charges
- Total Charges
- Internet Service
- Tech Support
- Churn Status

---

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1. Total Customers
Displays the total number of customers in the dataset.

### 2. Churn Distribution
Shows the proportion of customers who stayed versus customers who churned.

### 3. Contract Type vs Churn
Compares churn across different contract types (Month-to-Month, One Year, Two Year).

### 4. Monthly Charges Analysis
Illustrates how monthly charges relate to customer churn.

### 5. Tenure Analysis
Shows customer retention based on the length of their subscription.

### 6. Payment Method Analysis
Analyzes churn rates across different payment methods.

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** have the highest churn rate.
- Customers with **longer tenure** are more likely to remain with the company.
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn.
- Customers using **automatic payment methods** generally have better retention.
- Long-term contracts significantly improve customer loyalty.

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch from Month-to-Month to long-term contracts through discounts or incentives.
- Offer loyalty rewards for long-term customers.
- Improve customer support and service quality to reduce churn.
- Introduce personalized retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
- Promote automatic payment options to enhance customer retention.

---

## 📋 Conclusion

The Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard provides valuable insights into customer behavior and churn patterns. By identifying the factors contributing to customer loss, organizations can implement targeted strategies to improve retention, increase customer lifetime value, and drive long-term business growth.
